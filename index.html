Salguerro
Coastal Path

Those who walk this way will never forget their experience.


Each adventurer takes away something unique from
the Salguerro's balmy paths. For some, the thrill lies
in the arresting views. They wonder at the myriad
terrains one can tread while on a single coastal
path. For others, it's the breathtaking wildlife,
always within reach. Salguerro is, after all, home to
the rare Salgo Gulls and Gray-Crested Seabird. But
almost everybody will say that
it is the quiet that they won't forget - a comforting silence and solace
that cities can never give.


What will you take from the journey? Find out for
yourself. Salguerro awaits.

Croalstead Way

Those who walked Croalstead Way come
back a changed person. There's something
about the stillness up in those mountains
that calls to the quiet, forgotten corners of
our humanity. It brings memories from our
ancestors, those who first trod these
ancient landscapes - from a time before
time, when man lived with nature and her
seasons in perfect synchrony.

Croalstead Way is for serious hikers.
There are difficult treks and steep
passages that will require some
expertise and experience. Though
challenging, the reward of such a
precious experience is worth it. Not
many come this way, but those who do
find that the hike is a pilgrimage, and the
journey is within.
